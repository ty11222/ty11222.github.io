Takis on a Log

Description:

An unbelievably healthy and delicious snack classic, with an extra added kick!

Ingredients:

  • Celery sticks
  • Your favourite chewy peanut butter
  • A bag of red hot takis

How to prepare:

  1. Grab your celery sticks
  2. Spread peanut butter in the groves of the celery
  3. Carefully place your takis in the peanut butter
  4. Make sure they are placed carefully, if this previous step is messed up, all is lost...
