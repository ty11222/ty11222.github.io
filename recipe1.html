Healthy Snack Wrap

Description:

A healthy snack wrap filled with many delectable and healthy ingredients! You and all your roommates with love this on-the-go snack!

Ingredients:

  • Flour Tortilla
  • Your favourite chewey peanut butter
  • Jelly from your favourite fruit
  • Salted butter
  • Honey Nut Cherios

How to prepare:

  1. Warm your tortilla in the microwave for ~1 minute
  2. Spread the butter on one side of the tortilla
  3. Spread your peanut butter onto the other side of your tortilla
  4. Put jelly on top of the spreaded peanut butter
  5. Carefully place your honey nut cherios on top of all the previos ingredients
  6. Fold the tortilla like they do at Chipotle
  7. Enjoy this healthy snack on the go!
