Caesar Salad

Description:

The classic salad, loved by millions, made simple here.

Ingredients:

  • Lettuce Leaves
  • Crunchy Crutons
  • Caesar Dressing
  • A classic take-out container
  • A small plastic container with lid

How to prepare:

  1. Wash your whole lettuce leaves and put them into your dish (take-out container)
  2. Put your caesar dressing into your plastic container and put the lid on
  3. Put your crutons into the corner of your dish
  4. Enjoy putting your salad together!
